Daily reflection, tracked words and a supportive place for growth

About My Journal

My Journal is a private, simple and secure place for daily reflection. Use it to capture thoughts, track your mood over time, and reflect on growth. Entries and moods are stored in your Firebase project (Firestore) and accessible only to your account.

Core Features

  • ✅ Secure email/password authentication with Google OAuth
  • ✅ Create, edit and delete journal entries with rich text editor
  • ✅ Advanced mood tracker with analytics and history
  • ✅ PIN protection with customizable auto-lock
  • ✅ Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) with TOTP
  • ✅ Email verification for account changes
  • ✅ Responsive glassmorphism UI for mobile and desktop
  • ✅ Notifications: opt-in to receive broadcast messages
  • ✅ Beautiful loading animations and splash screens

Security & Privacy

Your entries are private and only accessible by your authenticated account. We support:

  • PIN-based app locking with customizable inactivity timers
  • Two-factor authentication via TOTP (Time-based One-Time Password)
  • Email verification for sensitive changes
  • Secure password reset and recovery
